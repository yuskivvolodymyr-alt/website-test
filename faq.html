QubeNode validator commission is 5% of network rewards. This is the lowest possible network commission. It is automatically deducted from accrued rewards, so you receive net rewards.

Calculation example:

You delegated 1000 coins. Network reward (APY) is 30% annual, so per year total network reward will be 300 coins. From this we deduct 5% validator commission:

300 coins - 5% commission = your net reward 285 coins (28.5%) and validator reward 15 coins (1.5%)

APY is 30% annually. However, it is worth noting that APY is a dynamic indicator and may change depending on various factors, including the total amount of staked coins in the network.
