📱 How to Delegate via Keplr Mobile

Step-by-step guide for delegating $TICS via Keplr mobile app:

1

Download Keplr Wallet from App Store or Play Store

2

Create new wallet

3

Securely save your seed phrase and password. Never share them with anyone

4

Add Qubetics Mainnet: find Qubetics and enable it

5

On main screen tap Deposit → find Qubetics → copy your Qubetics Mainnet wallet address (select EVM address, it starts with 0x...)

6

Send $TICS to copied address

7

When transaction completes and you see $TICS in your Keplr wallet, tap Qubetics Mainnet on main screen

8

Tap Stake
